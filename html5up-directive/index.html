Introducing: DAWN

Water scarcity is becoming increasingly prevalent, especially for residents of mobile homes and tiny homes, which often lack water infrastructure. Existing rainwater capture solutions are dependent on homes having gutters, large roof sizes, and need to be permanently and professionally installed, making them not accessible for mobile and tiny homes. DAWN can be easily installed by a consumer, is completely free-standing, and is easily collapsible. Additionally, customers that move can simply pack it up and reinstall the system at their next location.
